Smallbore Section

Small bore rifle

This match consists of 120 shots, 40 each in each of three positions, prone, standing, and kneeling.
It is fired with an elaborate .22 calibre Free Rifle. Accessories are commonly used for standing and kneeling stages.


Sporting rifle

Sporting Rifle is the most popular form of Smallbore rifle shooting in Canada.
Activities range from recreational shooting at local clubs, through inter-club leagues, and up to Provincial and Canadian Championships. Ordinary .22 calibre rifles are used.


Air rifle/pistol

Most people are surprised to find that good quality air rifles are the most accurate of all the firearms.
This popular event permits the use of any air or gas powered rifle have a bore of 4.5 mm (.177) and weight of no more that 5.5 kg (12.1 lbs)
