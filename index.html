Welcome To The Peterborough Fish and Game Association

The clubhouse is situated on 100 acres of property. Our facilities include a clubhouse with an indoor heated range, canteen, lounge, washrooms, 4 outdoor ranges for handguns, rifles, archery. The facilities are open 24 hours a day for club members. Out of consideration of our neighbours, the outdoor ranges shouldn't be used after dusk and the indoor ranges after 9 p.m. on weeknights and 9 p.m. on weekends or before noon on Sundays.

Check out the Schedule page for availability of the Indoor range

We have the following activities or sections - Archery, Handguns, Rifles, Air pistols, Air rifles, and Action Shooting

Our Junior program is second to none and has had Canadian and Ontario champions in air pistol, air rifle and sporting rifle.

The club also has a very active Action Shooting section with monthly competitions all year round They are outdoors during the nice weather then they move indoors for the winter.

The archery section is very active all year round (both indoors and outdoors with a 3-D walkabout range of over 20 targets. They have hosted many Canadian and Ontario events in conjunction with Saugeen Shafts.
