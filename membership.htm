Membership Info

Peterborough Fish & Game Association is currently under a temporary membership cap. If you are interested in joining the club you may come out as a guest three times to get to know the members and to find a sponsor for when membership opens up again.

We have sections that cater to all age groups.

Membership and Fee Structure - 2014 - 2015
Family or Full $275.00
Senior $255.00
Junior $175.00
University/College Associate (September to April) $175.00
 
Family or Full - Half Year (April to September 30) $175.00
Senior - Half Year (April to September 30) $155.00
Junior - Half Year (April to September 30) $115.00
    
Archery Associate (April to September 30) $175.00

For a full membership fee, you belong to all sections of the club.
A family membership includes your spouse, and all children under the age of 18 and still in school.
Memberships are reduced by 50% for new members who join between April and September.
Senior is anyone 65 years of age or over.
Junior membership is for individuals under the age of 18 whose parents are not members.

Membership applications are processed during the Executive meeting, the first tuesday of every month so get your applications in before then.
